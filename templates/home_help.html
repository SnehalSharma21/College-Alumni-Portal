Help & Support

How to Use This Website

  • Login / Register: New users click “Sign Up” and fill details. Existing users login with email & password.
  • View Events: Open the Events tab to see upcoming events and click “Register” to join.
  • Notices & Announcements: Check the Notices section for the latest updates.
  • Assignments & Notes: Download resources from the Assignments tab; upload your work if allowed.
  • Attendance: Go to the Attendance tab to view your attendance records.

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Forgot Password: Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
  • Website Loading Issues: Refresh, clear browser cache, or try another browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
  • Feature Access Problem: Some features depend on your role (Student, Alumni, Teacher, Admin). Contact support if you still face issues.
